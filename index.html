FAQ

Straight answers from people who run LIVE every day.

Do you work with new creators?

Yes. If you have 5k+ followers and 2+ posts/week, we can architect a LIVE system and provide hosts. For earlier‑stage creators, we offer an onboarding bundle with content sprints.

What brands are a fit?

Shop‑enabled brands or those ready to launch TikTok Shop. Categories we love: beauty, fashion, wellness, home, and electronics with bundles. We provide product positioning and offer design.

How fast to first LIVE?

Typical timeline is 7–14 days: audit, calendar, host training, and show 01. We can also plug into your existing schedule within 72 hours.

What’s included in production?

Run‑of‑show, overlays, hook library, UGC sourcing, offer architecture, real‑time A/B tests, comment moderation, and LIVE analytics. We can provide studio space and hosts in select cities.
